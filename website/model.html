How You Can Get Involved

Due to Global Pandemic outburst of COVID-19, we have suspended in-person volunteering/ internship engagements at present. Please apply if you are willing to work from home.

Corporate

Our internships are a great opportunity for learning. We offer you the chance to ignite your passion for positive action.In addition to volunteering with Udayan Care programmes, you can support our head office in areas like fund raising, communications, human resource management and research.

Internship

A mother's deep desire to educate her young daughter and build her a future,but her complete helplessness due to poverty, sparked the beginning of the UdayanShaliniProgramme.If you would like to join us as per personal interest and to gain experience, an application should be submitted in our format available on our website.

Mentors

Change the destiny of children, by becoming a guiding force that empowers them to dream.We invite individuals with a rich experience in parenting their own children to voluntarily commit themselves to bringing up children at Udayan Ghars.

International

Join our Team of Volunteers from 30 different Countries and make a lasting change.As an overseas volunteer at Udayan Care you would engage with the Udayan Ghar (homes) programme or the Udayan Shalini Fellowship programme (USF) and could also assist us in the office in streamlining processes.
